Welcome to my Flappy-owl game!

In this game you will use the Spacebar-key to flap the owl up and release the key to let the owl go down.
Too win you just have to flap the owl inbetween the poles,
when you lose is when you hit the poles.

The speed will go faster after every passage between the poles and your scores will also go up.
If you want to play more you just click on the restart button that comes up when you die.
Good luck!
